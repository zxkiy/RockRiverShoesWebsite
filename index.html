About Us

We Ensure The Best Experience.

Our company was established in 1998 with a multi brands shops offering gents & women’s shoes & accessories in the heart of Dubai, UAE. Most of the brands are from USA and Europe, as the company believes in the region’s quality of merchandise and the value it represents. The shop offers a wide range in the world- renounced brands of shoe collections. Rock River Shoes opened its first store in 2002. We currently have multiple stores both in Dubai. Our Stores feature an extensive collection of fashionable, contemporary footwear in line with changing trends. Products are sourced over from 3 countries and include accessories, leather products and jewelry to travel bags and shoe care products. More than 5 international Sports and Fashion Brands are listed in our portfolios.
